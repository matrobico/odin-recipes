Vegan Hamburger

Description

This is a simple, yet tasty recipe for a good ol' fashioned american burger. Vegan style!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beyond patties
  • Olive oil
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • A1 steak seasoning
  • Cheese slices

Steps

  1. Cook burgers for 4 minutes on each side in a pan filled with oil over medium heat.
  2. While burgers are cooking, season with A1 and place cheese slice on each patty.
  3. Toast buns
  4. Place cooked patties on buns
  5. Spread mayo
  6. Squirt ketchup
